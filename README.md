I like turning fuzzy ideas into working software: internal tools, dashboards, APIs, AI experiments, and web products that people can actually use. My work sits around full-stack development, backend systems, practical AI, and data-heavy workflows.

These days, most of my projects orbit around:

- LLM systems with routing, budgets, fallback behavior, testing, and observability.
- Full-stack AI apps that use voice, vision, real-time APIs, and accessible UX.
- Cloud and data tools: dashboards, ETL, validation, automation, and internal platforms.
- Engineering fundamentals: APIs, distributed systems, databases, reliability, and security.
